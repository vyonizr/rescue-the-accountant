rescue the accountant

     You are Raiden Pearce, a highly skilled grey hat hacker. Your old partner Mason Bourne asks for your help to find someone named Christian Wolf.

    Wolf, an accountant who's also a former SIA (State Intelligence Agency) cryptanalyst, is currently being hunted by an unknown mercenary organization. Your job is to help Bourne by rescuing Wolf to a hideout without raising any suspicions. Latest intel said that Wolf is working at a building called "42 Exchange", a place where very high employee mobility happens. However, he always stays on the same window side as the main entrance of the building.

    As usual, Bourne uses his sharp skills to infiltrate the building where Wolf is working at by disguising himself as one of the security guards. With your hacking capabilities, you're able to access all of the surveillance cameras and got their feeds below. Tell Bourne which floor and which window where Wolf is located. In order to decrease the possibility of decrypted transmissions, Wolf is assigned with codename "vertical bar".

As agreed with Bourne, the ground floor starts from 1 and window 1 starts from the left. Everyone you see now would be different if you restart your feed.
